About

I am currently a Ph.D. scholar at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Bhopal, India, where I work at the intersection of learning and control for autonomous vehicles. My research focuses on autonomous navigation in multi-agent systems using data-driven reinforcement learning and model predictive control.

I am developing deep reinforcement learning algorithms that help autonomous vehicles make well-ordered control decisions in complex, dynamic environments involving multiple agents. My work addresses challenges such as static and dynamic obstacle handling, real-time decision-making, and safer autonomous behavior in real-world scenarios.

More broadly, I am interested in building reliable, adaptive, and safe intelligent systems for future mobility.
